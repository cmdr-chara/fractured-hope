# Chara and HChara

[[characters/hchara|HChara]] is not a stranger to [[characters/chara|Chara]]. She is an alternate self from Timeline 284: a version of Chara whose world was already dead by the time she escaped the experiments.

## The Warning

HChara shows Chara a possible endpoint. She is what grief, violence, and isolation can become when every human restraint has already failed.

That is why their fight matters beyond combat. HChara's argument is dangerous because it is built from material Chara recognizes.

## The Mercy Test

HChara asks Chara to kill her. Her reason is that she wants to see Raiden one more time.

Chara refuses. This is one of the story's hardest mercy choices because HChara is asking sincerely and is not offered a simple redemption path.

## Afterward

Kager later kills HChara and shatters both her soul and Lumina. Her personal death is confirmed; what remains unresolved is the long-term fate of the shattered Lumina.
