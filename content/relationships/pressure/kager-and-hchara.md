# Kager and HChara

[[characters/kager|Kager]] deploys [[characters/hchara|HChara]] in Arc 5 as a weapon against [[characters/chara|Chara]]. Their relationship is not partnership. It is use.

## What Kager Uses

HChara is valuable to Kager because she is an alternate Chara shaped by a dead route. She embodies the conclusion Kager wants Chara to accept: that hope only prolongs failure and that a perfect replacement world is preferable to broken people.

## The Discard

After HChara fails to resolve the conflict, Kager kills her and shatters both her soul and Lumina. That act clarifies his relationship to the tools he creates: personhood stops mattering once usefulness ends.
