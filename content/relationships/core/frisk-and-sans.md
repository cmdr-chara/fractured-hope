# Frisk and Sans

[[characters/frisk|Frisk]] and [[characters/sans|Sans]] are tied by shared culpability before they are tied by family. Both are present in the history of [[characters/chara|Chara]]'s Library containment: Sans manifests the Judgment Hall and crushes Chara's soul; Frisk locks the door.

## Shared History

Their connection matters because the story does not isolate guilt into one person. The Library sequence is institutional, procedural, and personal at the same time. Frisk and Sans each carry a different part of that history.

## Present-Day Household

By the main story, both are part of the household around Chara. The fact that they remain present does not erase the Library. It makes the household more complicated.

## Arc 6 Grief

After Frisk is killed in Arc 5, Sans is the one in the kitchen making pie. He cannot say her name directly. The scene makes their connection visible through absence: grief handled by doing an ordinary task badly enough that the silence becomes the point.
