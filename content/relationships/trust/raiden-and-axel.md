# Raiden and Axel

[[characters/raiden|Raiden]] and [[characters/axel|Axel]] are connected through hidden research, reset-resistant memory, and the fusion plan that later reaches [[characters/chara|Chara]].

## Covert Collaboration

Axel worked covertly with Raiden and retained access to the hidden lab. His synthesized Determination micro-dosing lets him preserve core memories across timeline shifts, which makes him one of the few people capable of carrying Raiden's plan forward.

## The Plan After Raiden

After Raiden is removed from the story, Axel becomes the surviving gatekeeper to:

- the hidden lab
- the soul fusion procedure
- artificial soul matrix research
- Raiden's anti-Kager preparations
- Chara's stabilization notes

This makes their relationship structurally important even though Raiden is absent for most of the narrative.

## Unresolved

> **Unresolved:** Axel's exact contribution to the artificial soul matrix work and the limits of his loyalty are not confirmed.
