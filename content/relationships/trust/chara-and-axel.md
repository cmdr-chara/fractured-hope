# Chara and Axel

[[characters/axel|Axel]] is the person who gives [[characters/chara|Chara]] access to [[characters/raiden|Raiden]]'s hidden research and administers the fusion that changes the rest of the story. Their relationship is useful, necessary, and never fully clean.

## Gatekeeper to Raiden's Work

Axel preserves enough memory across resets to keep Raiden's plan alive. When Chara reaches the hidden lab, Axel is the one who can explain what Raiden built and what the fusion will cost.

That gives him a strange role: he is not Raiden, but much of Raiden's surviving plan reaches Chara through him.

## The Fusion

Axel administers the soul fusion and warns Chara before the final Hate injection severs her reset anchor. He does not hide the cost, but he still performs the procedure.

This puts him in one of the story's most uncomfortable positions: the person helping Chara survive is also the person making survival irreversible.

## Unresolved Trust

Axel's possible betrayal, his overlap with Kager imagery, and his exact role in the artificial soul matrix work remain unresolved. The relationship therefore stays suspended between trust and suspicion.
