# Liberado

Liberado is the resistance organization at the center of _Fractured Hope_'s present-day story. It was built in [[locations/brasilia|Brasilia]] by [[characters/chara|Chara]] and [[characters/raiden|Raiden]], and it remains Chara's base of operations after Raiden's [[lore/lumina|Lumina]] is fragmented in the Prologue.

## Role in the Story

Liberado is both a military and emotional structure. It is where Chara leads, where people like [[characters/frisk|Frisk]] work beside her, and where Raiden's absence becomes impossible to ignore. The group exists inside an active war, so even its moments of normalcy are temporary and under pressure.

## Founding and Leadership

Chara and Raiden founded Liberado together during the years between Chara's escape from captivity and the opening of the story. By the main timeline, Chara commands it and Raiden is remembered as its lead researcher and one of the people who shaped its deeper metaphysical work.

## Known Members

The current wiki material directly ties these characters to Liberado:

- [[characters/chara|Chara]] — commander
- [[characters/raiden|Raiden]] — co-founder and lead researcher
- [[characters/frisk|Frisk]] — field medic

Other close allies move around its orbit, but the exact institutional boundaries of membership are not always spelled out.

## Narrative Importance

Liberado matters because it gives the story something larger than personal grief to protect. Chara is not fighting only for revenge or recovery. She is also trying to hold together a living community inside a war-damaged city.

## Open Questions

- How large Liberado is, beyond the named cast, is still unclear.
- Its exact structure, resources, and chain of command are not described in full.
- The boundary between Liberado's aid role and its military role remains only partially defined.
