# Sans

Sans occupies one of the hardest positions in _Fractured Hope_. He helped confine [[characters/chara|Chara]], crushed her soul in the Judgment Hall, and then spent years trying to protect her without pretending that damage never happened.

## Overview

In this story, Sans is less comic relief than burdened witness. He knows what institutions did to Chara, knows what she is capable of, and knows what [[characters/raiden|Raiden]] asked of him before he disappeared.

## Background

### The Library and the Judgment Hall

Sans is part of the Library sequence after Chara's escape from Lab C7. He manifests the Judgment Hall inside the white-room containment site and crushes her soul while Frisk locks the door. That moment is one of the deepest breaks in Chara's history.

### Raiden's request

Later, Sans reveals that Raiden made him promise to watch Chara if things collapsed. The promise is protective but limited: he is meant to help her, not take control of her life.

## Personality

Sans remains casual on the surface, but the story treats that tone as cover rather than lightness. He is tired, careful, emotionally indirect, and still trying to be useful after having once helped do irreversible harm.

## Abilities

- telekinesis
- soul-targeting attacks
- Judgment Hall manifestation
- high-level combat ability
- timeline sensitivity

He is powerful enough to matter in direct conflict, but he deliberately holds back in the present because of the promise he made to Raiden.

## Relationships

- **[[characters/chara|Chara]]** — part of one of the worst moments in her history, later one of her few dependable protectors
- **[[characters/raiden|Raiden]]** — the person whose request defines Sans's role in the main story
- **[[characters/frisk|Frisk]]** — tied to the same Library/Judgment Hall history
- **[[characters/paloma|Paloma]]** — indirectly linked through the revolver he delivers before Arc 6's closing battle

## Story

### Past

Sans is part of the institutional violence done to Chara in the Library.

### Middle arcs

He stays near the household, watches for danger, and performs quiet acts of care such as checking on Chara, bringing food, and holding the line without trying to dominate her choices.

### Arc 6

Before the closing battle, Sans delivers Paloma's finished revolver and hugs Chara before she leaves. The story plays both moments straight.

## Open Questions

- How much did Sans know before the Library sequence fully unfolded?
- What is the full limit of his promise to Raiden?
- Can present-day care ever meaningfully coexist with what he did in the past?
