# HChara

HChara is an alternate Chara from Timeline 284, deployed by [[characters/kager|Kager]] in Arc 5. She is both an opponent and a warning: a version of Chara shaped by ruin, grief, and the total surrender of hope.

## Overview

HChara is not a simple evil double. She matters because her argument is built from the same emotional material as Chara's own life, pushed further and broken harder.

## Timeline 284

According to HChara, her timeline was already effectively dead by the time she escaped experimentation. She killed everyone involved, including Raiden, and Kager offered her a perfect replacement world.

That history makes her less a mirror and more a failed endpoint.

## Appearance

HChara's imagery is among the most striking in the story:

- mirror-like eyes
- clean tears on one side of the face and dark blood on the other
- a presence that feels consumed rather than merely empowered

## Fight with Chara

Her fight with Chara is ideological as much as physical. She argues that hope only prolongs suffering, and that argument matters because it comes from lived collapse rather than empty cynicism.

She is also one of the few characters who immediately understands what Chara's HATE manifestation means.

## Death

After Chara refuses to kill her, Kager does it himself. He severs her head and destroys both soul and Lumina, proving that even complete submission to his logic does not make anyone safe inside it.
