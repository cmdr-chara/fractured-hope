# Frisk

Frisk is [[Liberado]]'s field medic and one of the people closest to [[characters/chara|Chara]] in the main story. She is also directly tied to some of the worst violence in Chara's past: she knew about the imprisonment, participated in the Library sequence, and later confesses that she watched Chara's suffering through a one-way mirror and took notes.

Kager kills her in Arc 5 to wound Chara more deeply. Even then, she does not disappear entirely. Her [[lore/determination|Determination]] lets her persist as a resonance form during the regeneration window that carries into Arc 6.

## Overview

Frisk matters because the story refuses to simplify her. She is healer, witness, accomplice, family, and ghost all at once. Her relationship with Chara is not cleaned up into innocence or total condemnation. The story keeps all of it alive.

## Background

### Early involvement in Chara's captivity

Frisk is present around the time of Chara's abduction and knows about what is being done to her. After Chara escapes Lab C7, Frisk meets her in the forest and admits she already knew about the imprisonment.

She then participates in the transfer to the Grand Library. During that period, she is present for the Judgment Hall sequence, locks the door, and later admits that she observed Chara through a one-way mirror and took notes on her screams.

### Main-story role

By the present timeline, Frisk works inside Liberado as a field medic. The years between the Library and the main story are still only partially explained.

## Appearance

Frisk has short black hair with purple threads, darker eyes than she had when younger, and a pearlescent white tactical medical vest. She carries a Kindness emitter and a Wasp Knife.

## Personality

Frisk is direct when she finally chooses to speak, but she is also someone who waited far too long to tell the truth. She is supportive, medically skilled, and capable of quiet care, yet her history with Chara prevents the story from ever treating that care as clean.

## Abilities

- **Determination** — weaker than Chara's, but strong enough to let her persist after death
- **Healing** — her primary functional specialty
- **Kindness emitter** — support/combat tool with partially unclear mechanics
- **Wasp Knife** — melee weapon
- **Self-regeneration** — unusually strong
- **Heartbeat detection** — approximately 100-meter range

## Relationships

- **[[characters/chara|Chara]]** — former handler, later family-like companion, and the person most deeply shaped by Frisk's confession
- **[[characters/kager|Kager]]** — he kills her as a tactical strike against Chara
- **[[characters/sans|Sans]]** — tied to the same Judgment Hall history, though the full shape of their relationship is still unclear

## Story

### Before the main plot

Frisk is already entangled in Chara's imprisonment history and institutional surveillance.

### Present timeline

She serves as Liberado's medic and stays close to Chara, carrying years of unspoken guilt.

### Arc 4.4

Frisk confesses everything, including the one-way-mirror detail. She does not ask for easy forgiveness, and Chara does not cut her off.

### Arc 5 and Arc 6

Kager kills Frisk in Arc 5 to weaken Chara directly. Her Determination lets her continue in a partial, intangible resonance form during the two-day regeneration window, and she remains present as a witness during the closing Arc 6 sequence.

## Status

Frisk is killed in Arc 5, but not erased. During Arc 6 she persists in a partial resonance state while her regeneration window remains active: unable to act physically, but still present and perceptible.
