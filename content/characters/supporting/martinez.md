# Martinez

Martinez is one of the three named members of the Arc 6 assault unit. She is the only member of the trio whose death is clearly confirmed during the closing battle.

## Overview

Martinez's role is narrow but important. Her death makes the Arc 6 fight feel concrete and costly instead of purely transitional on the way to the confrontation with [[characters/elias-the-administrator|Elias the Administrator]].

## Role in the Story

Martinez fights alongside [[characters/marcus|Marcus]] and [[characters/supporting/ale|Ale]] in the ruined-city assault sequence. The story confirms that she is killed during the confrontation with [[characters/chara|Chara]].

## Combat Context

Like the rest of the unit, Martinez operates in disciplined kill-box terrain across rooftops, sewers, basements, and ruined streets. The story does not cleanly isolate her personal fighting style or assign her a confirmed individual weapon set.

## Status

Martinez is dead by the end of the battle sequence, making her the clearest casualty among the three named Arc 6 militants.
