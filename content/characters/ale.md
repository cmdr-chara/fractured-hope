# Ale

Ale is one of the three named members of the Arc 6 assault unit alongside [[characters/marcus|Marcus]] and [[characters/martinez|Martinez]]. She is part of the disciplined militant force that fights Chara in the ruined lower city before the confrontation shifts toward [[characters/elias-the-administrator|Elias the Administrator]].

## Overview

Ale is the least individually defined member of the trio, but she still matters for two reasons. First, her presence helps make the Arc 6 assault feel like a real organized cell rather than a mass of anonymous enemies. Second, the fact that the manuscript names her but does not fully resolve her creates a deliberate asymmetry inside the trio: Martinez is the confirmed casualty, Marcus is the unresolved survivor, and Ale remains present without receiving a clean endpoint.

## Role in Arc 6

Ale fights in the same urban kill-box environment as the rest of the assault cell. The confirmed battle space includes ruined streets, rooftops, sewers, and basements inside war-damaged [[locations/brasilia|Brasilia]]. This is a coordinated combat zone, not incidental scenery. The unit uses the terrain as structure.

Ale's page is therefore less about a fully separate biography and more about tactical placement. She is one of the people who turns the last battle's opening phase into a disciplined hunt.

## Combat Context

The reviewed source material does not assign Ale an exclusive named weapon or unique signature ability. What can be said cleanly is that she operates inside the same combat language as the rest of the cell:

- coordinated movement across layered terrain
- participation in the same disciplined assault pattern as Marcus and Martinez
- presence during the five-second phase of Chara's Arc 6 brutality

That last point matters. Ale is part of the group against whom the manuscript frames Chara's line:

> _"Heads, you die. Tails... you get a five-second head start."_

The scene gives Ale narrative weight even without isolating her into a full personal dossier.

## Status

Ale remains explicitly present late enough in the Arc 6 sequence that the wiki cannot list her as dead. Unlike Martinez, she is not a confirmed casualty in the reviewed material. Unlike Marcus, her page is not centered on a specifically flagged unresolved death question. Her status remains open.

## What Is Known and Unknown

Known:

- Ale is a named member of the assault unit.
- She fights in the Arc 6 lower-city sequence.
- She is still textually present beyond Martinez's confirmed death.

Unknown:

- whether "Ale" is her full name
- what her exact specialty inside the unit was
- whether she survives the battle in the long term
- what higher command structure she serves under
