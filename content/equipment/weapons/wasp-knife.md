# Wasp Knife

The wasp knife is a weapon in form, but its main story function is as a message. [[characters/raiden|Raiden]] gave it to [[characters/frisk|Frisk]] years before the main story with instructions to deliver it to [[characters/chara|Chara]] if he was not there.

## Raiden's Instruction

Frisk explains the handoff plainly:

> _"He gave it to me. Years ago. Before everything. He said, 'If I'm not there to save her, give her this. It's the only thing that can remind her who she is.'"_

The knife therefore carries trust across several damaged relationships at once: Raiden trusting Frisk, Frisk carrying the burden, and Chara receiving a message from someone she is losing from memory.

## Arc 5 Message

The embedded message is the story's clearest refusal of Kager's perfection logic:

> _"If you're hearing this, you're about to become me. Don't. Choose the pain. Choose the failure. Choose them."_

The knife does not solve the crisis. It gives Chara a sentence strong enough to stand on while she refuses the perfect path.
