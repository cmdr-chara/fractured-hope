# Pendant and Red Knife

The pendant is an artifact that exists in two states: its carried form (a pendant) and its transformed state (a red knife). The transformation is confirmed in the Arc 6 source material. Both states are the same object.

## The Transformation

The pendant's transformation into the red knife occurs under specific conditions during Arc 6. The source material confirms the transformation takes place but does not detail the precise trigger mechanism.

> **Unresolved:** The specific trigger and conditions for the pendant's transformation into the red knife are not detailed in reviewed sources. Whether it requires intent, emotional threshold, soul-state threshold, or a specific activation is not confirmed.

## The Red Knife

As the red knife, the artifact is a combat weapon. Its presence in Arc 6 is consistent with the closing battle's escalation. The red color carries the same thematic register as [[lore/determination|Determination]]'s red soul association and the [[lore/the-red-genocide|Red Genocide]] classification — red in this story's visual language marks things of dangerous significance.

## Carried Form

In its pendant form, the artifact is a personal object — something worn or kept rather than drawn. The pendant-as-carried-form suggests the object has a significance beyond combat, functioning as an anchor or marker in ordinary life before becoming a weapon under pressure.

This pattern is consistent with how _Fractured Hope_ treats personal objects generally: the locket, the lily note, the pendant all have an everyday-presence form and a moment-of-crisis form.
