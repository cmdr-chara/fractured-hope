# Justice Revolver

The Justice revolver is the firearm [[characters/paloma|Paloma]] completes before the Arc 6 closing battle. [[characters/sans|Sans]] delivers it to [[characters/chara|Chara]] before she leaves.

## Function

The weapon appears in the final combat stretch as part of Chara's Arc 6 kit. The reviewed material confirms a **Justice revolver execution shot**, but does not fully document the weapon's construction, ammunition, or limits.

## Paloma's Role

Paloma's work on the revolver matters because she is otherwise largely off-page in Arc 6. The weapon is her most concrete contribution to the closing fight after Javier's death and her Arc 5 transformation.

## Sans's Delivery

Sans delivering the revolver links the weapon to his eight-year promise to Raiden: do not interfere unless Chara is about to bring the whole house down. The delivery sits beside the brief hug before Chara leaves.

## Unresolved

> **Unresolved:** The exact technical mechanism of the Justice revolver is not detailed in reviewed sources.
