# Model 500 Blueprint

The Model 500 blueprint is an artifact produced by [[characters/chara|Chara]] — a technical document that is also an expression of her [[lore/creation-magic|creation-magic]] capacity. The manuscript confirms its existence in the pre-story material, where it sits alongside the [[lore/raiden-soul-stabilization-notes|stabilization notes]], [[lore/lily-note|lily note]], and other objects that carry significant narrative weight.

## What It Is

The blueprint is a design document — a technical schematic for something Chara created. The exact nature of what is being blueprinted (a weapon, a device, a construct) is not fully detailed in reviewed sources. What is confirmed is that it is a product of her creation impulse rather than a combat ability or institutional deliverable.

> **Unresolved:** What the Model 500 blueprint describes — what the "Model 500" is — is not confirmed in detail in reviewed sources.

## Creation Magic Context

The blueprint's significance in the source material is partly about what it represents: evidence that Chara creates. The [[lore/lab-c7|Lab C7]] program and the [[lore/the-red-genocide|Red Genocide]] classification are entirely about what she can do to the institution. The Model 500 blueprint is about what she does for herself and for the people she chooses.

This connects it to the broader [[lore/creation-magic|creation magic]] thread. The obsession with creating rather than only destroying is documented as a pre-story character property. The blueprint is its most concrete material form.

## Placement in the Artifact Cluster

The Model 500 blueprint appears in the same source material range as the locket, the lily note, and the stabilization notes — the pre-story and Arc 4 period. It is one of several objects that anchor the period before the main story through material evidence of who Raiden and Chara were to each other and to their work.
