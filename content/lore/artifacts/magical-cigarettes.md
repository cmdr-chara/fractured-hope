# Magical Cigarettes

Magical cigarettes are objects in _Fractured Hope_ with properties that extend beyond ordinary tobacco. They appear in the source material as part of the character texture of the story — present in the world, associated with specific figures, and carrying a quiet implication that even mundane-seeming objects can be imbued with something more.

## Confirmed Properties

The manuscript confirms magical cigarettes exist and are used by characters in the story's world. The specific properties — what the magic does, whether it is protective, stimulant-like, calming, or something else — are not detailed in reviewed sources.

> **Unresolved:** The exact magical properties of the cigarettes are not documented in detail in reviewed sources.

## Character Association

The cigarettes appear in connection with characters who operate in the story's combat and espionage register. They are not major-plot artifacts — they do not have a dedicated scene or transformation moment — but their presence contributes to the texture of a world where magic has permeated ordinary life, including the most casual habits.

> **Unresolved:** Which specific characters use or carry magical cigarettes is not confirmed in the source material reviewed for this wiki.

## Thematic Register

The cigarettes occupy the space where _Fractured Hope_'s magical world and its ordinary human activities intersect. A character lighting something magical in a quiet moment is a mundane gesture with an extraordinary substrate. This minor-artifact register is part of how the story keeps its world from feeling entirely defined by its most extreme events.
