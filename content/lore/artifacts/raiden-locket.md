# Raiden's Locket

Raiden's locket is one of the primary material anchors of [[characters/raiden|Raiden]]'s presence in _Fractured Hope_. He is dead before the main story begins. The locket, along with his [[lore/raiden-soul-stabilization-notes|soul-stabilization notes]] and the [[lore/lily-note|lily note]], is one of the objects through which he remains a functional part of the story.

## The Scent-Signature

The source material confirms that the locket carries Raiden's scent-signature. This is not incidental — in a story where [[lore/manifested-notes|manifested notes]] and physically-present objects carry soul-weight beyond their literal content, the locket's scent-signature marks it as a contact point rather than just a memento.

The beach sequence confirms the locket's presence in the same emotional register as Raiden's scent, the lily note, and the first embrace. The manuscript clusters these objects and sensations together as a way of rendering his absence as something that occupies space.

## Connection to the Reset Anchor

The relationship between the locket and the [[timeline/reset-sequence|reset anchor]] Raiden built for Chara is not fully confirmed. The anchor is described as a _"dim yellow light"_ — possibly tied to the locket, possibly separate. The soul-stabilization notes are another candidate for the anchor's physical form.

> **Unresolved:** Whether the reset anchor Raiden built is housed in or tied to the locket is not confirmed in reviewed sources.

## Arc 4 Presence

In Arc 4, the locket is part of the material texture of Raiden's remembered presence. It appears in connection with the flashback material — the first embrace, the beach, the period when Raiden and Chara were in the same physical space. By Arc 4, that period is memory; the locket is what remains.
