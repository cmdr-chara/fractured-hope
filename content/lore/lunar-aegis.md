# Lunar Aegis

Lunar Aegis is [[characters/valerius|Valerius]]'s primary defensive combat ability. It is confirmed in the Arc 6 source material by its invocation:

> _"LUNAR AEGIS!"_

The ability is moon-aligned and functions as a large-scale barrier. It is deployed in the Arc 6 closing battle during the confrontation with the assault unit and the subsequent engagement with [[characters/elias-the-administrator|Elias]].

## Mechanics

Lunar Aegis creates a defensive field with significant coverage. The source material confirms it is deployed against heavy offensive pressure — the Arc 6 battle involves a disciplined assault cell and escalating engagement, and Valerius's barrier role is a defined tactical function alongside Chara's offense.

The ability is named and deliberate, not a passive property. Valerius calls it explicitly, suggesting intentional activation rather than ambient defense.

## Relationship to Penumbra

Lunar Aegis and [[lore/penumbra|Penumbra]] are Valerius's paired abilities — defensive and offensive/shadow aspects of the same lunar-based combat kit. They are documented together in the Arc 6 material. The manuscript does not fully detail how they interact or whether using one affects the availability of the other.

> **Unresolved:** The energy cost and recovery time for Lunar Aegis are not specified in reviewed sources.

> **Unresolved:** Whether Lunar Aegis can be sustained over extended periods or requires re-activation is not confirmed.

## Eclipse Interaction

The Arc 6 material confirms that [[lore/eclipse-and-lunar-entropy|Eclipse / Lunar Entropy]] is active during the closing battle. Valerius's lunar abilities operate within this context. Whether the eclipse state strengthens, weakens, or modifies Lunar Aegis is not explicitly confirmed, but the pairing of a lunar defender and an active eclipse event is not treated as coincidental.
