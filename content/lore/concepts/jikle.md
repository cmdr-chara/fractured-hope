# Jikle

Jikle is an artificial soul construct formed from [[lore/determination|Determination]] and Hatred. The story identifies it as **The Soul of Unmaking**.

## What It Is

Jikle is not one of the nine natural soul types. It is the result of forced fusion between two states that should not coexist cleanly: the persistence of Determination and the annihilative drive of Hate.

The story treats that combination as fundamentally unstable.

## Known Properties

- associated with [[characters/kager|Kager]]
- capable of erasing [[lore/lumina|Lumina]] fragments across realities
- described as something that should kill its user by the logic the story establishes for soul fusion
- still somehow usable by Kager

## Narrative Importance

Jikle matters because it is the metaphysical shape of Kager's worldview. It does not preserve. It does not heal. It does not even simply overpower. It removes.

That makes it the worst possible counterforce to Raiden's fragmented Lumina and to any hope of repair.

## Unresolved

> **Unresolved:** The manuscript does not yet explain why Jikle does not kill Kager, whether it is unique, or whether it can be reproduced.
