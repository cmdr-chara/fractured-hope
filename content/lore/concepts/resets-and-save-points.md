# Resets and Save Points

Resets and save points are part of _Fractured Hope_'s timeline logic. They matter early as a fallback structure and later because that fallback is taken away.

## Reset Logic

The handoff summary and Arc 2 material establish the broad rules:

- Chara uses Raiden's reset contingency after the initial loss
- Determination is the soul property most closely tied to timeline retention and continuity
- resets are not free; they preserve damage and grief in altered form rather than restoring innocence

## Save Points and Anchors

The story distinguishes ordinary endurance from a deeper tether back to a survivable state. That tether is often referred to in the wiki as the **reset anchor**.

Raiden builds a final reset contingency specifically for Chara, described in the text as a dim yellow light meant for the moment when she would be alone and broken.

## Permanent Loss

During the final Hate fusion, Axel confirms that fusion severs timeline anchors permanently.

> _"No resets, no reloads. This is your only life now."_

That line changes the structure of the story. After this point, consequences stop being provisional.

## SAVE Star

The flashback material references a SAVE Star found in the Library data room. Chara takes it during the confrontation that follows her escape from Lab C7. The exact mechanics of that object are not fully documented in the current published pages.
