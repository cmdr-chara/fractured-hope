# Determination

Determination is one of nine documented [[lore/souls|soul types]] in _Fractured Hope_. In high concentrations it allows a person to retain memory and intent across reset events, resist soul-level manipulation, and survive conditions the manuscript describes as extinction-level.

## Definition

The story treats Determination not as a personality trait, but as a structural property of certain souls. It describes what a soul can do under sufficient stress rather than what a person is. Chara's Determination is high enough that [[lore/lab-c7|Lab C7]] researchers assigned her the codename **THE RED GENOCIDE**, representing their calculated extinction probability if she were left unchecked.

Determination is not stability. The manuscript shows Chara's Determination enabling survival while simultaneously making certain kinds of peace harder to reach — the same property that holds memory across resets also holds damage.

## Known Rules

- Determination allows retention of memory through reset events, where lower-DT individuals lose continuity.
- It can be partially laced into external objects — the abduction dart used on Chara at sixteen was Perseverance-laced rather than Determination-laced, suggesting targeted suppression was needed precisely because her Determination resisted direct interference.
- Soul fusion with a Determination-type soul carries irreversible consequences; the reset anchor is severed permanently during Arc 2.3.
- Determination does not protect against [[lore/lumina|Lumina]] damage. Lumina fragmentation is distinct from soul injury and follows different rules.

## The Tenth Type: Jikle

Jikle is described in the manuscript as an impossible fusion of Hatred and Determination — a tenth type that should not exist within the documented nine-type framework. Its mechanics are not fully explained.

> **Unresolved:** The manuscript does not specify whether Jikle can be replicated or whether it is unique to a single individual.

## Narrative Importance

Determination is the axis around which _Fractured Hope_'s central argument turns. [[characters/kager|Kager]]'s position is that systems running on love are designed to fail; the story's answer runs through a character whose Determination is measurable, documented, and feared — and who chooses, in Arc 5, to accept loss rather than optimize it away.

The extinction probability file does not measure whether Chara will survive. It measures what the government believed she would do to them if she did.

## Examples in the Text

- Chara retains full memory of Timeline 284 events that HChara experienced.
- The reset anchor, Raiden's final gift, is built specifically for her Determination level — described as "a dim yellow light" intended for "the moment when she would be alone and broken."
- After the anchor is severed, Chara continues without the option of return. The manuscript treats this as a permanent narrowing, not a loss of the underlying trait.
