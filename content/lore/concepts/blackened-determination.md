# Blackened Determination

Blackened Determination is the form [[lore/determination|Determination]] takes under HATE contamination or eclipse-state entropic pressure. It appears in the Arc 6 closing battle material as a distinct combat state — Determination operating at full destructive output but with the stability and precision properties of normal Determination degraded.

## What Changes

Normal Determination in _Fractured Hope_ carries two properties in tension: its persistence-across-failure quality, and its capacity for fine-grained control. Blackened Determination prioritizes the former at the expense of the latter. It is Determination that has absorbed enough HATE or [[lore/eclipse-and-lunar-entropy|Lunar Entropy]] that it is no longer operating cleanly — it persists, it forces, it binds, but it is less controlled.

This is the state from which the [[lore/soul-tether|soul tether]] is built. The tether's HATE component and the Determination component are not neatly separable in this state; the blackening is what makes the combination possible.

## Manifestation

The manuscript's confirmation of blackened Determination as a distinct state comes from the Arc 6 material. It is not described in extensive mechanic detail — it appears as a visible quality of [[characters/chara|Chara]]'s combat state during the closing confrontation with [[characters/elias-the-administrator|Elias]].

> _"Don't look away, Frisk… I'm just getting warm."_

The line suggests escalation — a state that is building rather than at ceiling, and that has moved past the point of ordinary Determination control.

## Relationship to the Red Genocide Classification

The institution's fear of Chara was based on what she might do with unconstrained Determination. Blackened Determination represents the version of that fear that is closest to realized: Determination still directed by her intent, but degraded enough that precision is not guaranteed. The [[lore/the-red-genocide|Red Genocide]] classification was a probability calculation; blackened Determination is the operational state that makes that calculation feel close.

## Open Questions

> **Unresolved:** Whether blackened Determination is a temporary state that can be reversed or a permanent alteration to her soul properties is not confirmed in reviewed sources.

> **Unresolved:** Whether the eclipse state is necessary to produce blackened Determination, or whether HATE contamination alone is sufficient, is not confirmed.
