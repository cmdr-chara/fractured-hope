# Artificial Soul Matrices

Artificial soul matrices are experimental constructs developed in the _Fractured Hope_ universe to replicate or approximate natural soul properties. The source material confirms their existence as part of the Lab C7 and related research apparatus. Raiden's records address them, and they appear in the guilt and confession material associated with Frisk and the institution.

## What They Are

A soul matrix is a structured replication of the functional properties of a natural soul — the capacity to carry Determination, Perseverance, or other soul-type properties in a manufactured substrate. The research context is Lab C7, where artificial matrices are connected to projects aimed at understanding, controlling, or weaponizing soul-type mechanics.

The source material confirms the research exists and that Raiden engaged with it in his notes, but does not fully detail the mechanism of construction or the limits of what an artificial matrix can replicate.

## Relationship to Natural Souls

Natural [[lore/souls|souls]] in _Fractured Hope_ carry inherent properties determined by type and the individual's history. An artificial matrix attempts to replicate those properties without the organic origin. The manuscript does not confirm that artificial matrices are functionally equivalent to natural souls — the gap between replication and original is treated as significant.

The connection to [[lore/soul-fusion|soul fusion]] is noted: the research into artificial matrices runs alongside the research that eventually produces the fusion outcomes in Arc 2. Whether artificial matrices were intended as a safer alternative to direct fusion is not confirmed.

## Research Context

The institutional motivation for artificial soul matrix research is consistent with the Lab C7 program's broader goal: reduce dependence on specific individuals with dangerous soul properties (like Chara) by replicating those properties in controllable substrates. The [[lore/the-red-genocide|Red Genocide]] classification is part of the same logic — fear of uncontrolled Determination driving a search for controlled alternatives.

## Open Questions

> **Unresolved:** Whether any functional artificial soul matrix was successfully constructed during the manuscript's timeline is not confirmed in reviewed sources.

> **Unresolved:** The relationship between artificial soul matrices and Raiden's soul-stabilization notes is not fully mapped. Whether the notes describe matrix construction, stabilization of natural souls, or both is not clear.
