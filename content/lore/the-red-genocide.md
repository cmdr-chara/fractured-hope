# The Red Genocide

**The Red Genocide** is the codename assigned to [[characters/chara|Chara]] in the Lab C7 subject profile generated from live stress-test analysis.

## The File

The flashback sequence gives the relevant lines directly:

```text
SUBJECT PROFILE: C-1 (CHARA)
SOURCE OF DATA: LIVE STRESS TEST ANALYSIS (LAB C7)
EXTINCTION PROBABILITY: 99.4%
CODENAME: THE RED GENOCIDE
```

## Meaning

The phrase matters because it is not just military shorthand. It is the story's clearest institutional judgment on Chara: that she is best understood as a civilization-level risk model before she is understood as a person.

The number follows her through the entire narrative.

## Narrative Importance

The codename condenses several major themes into one document:

- trauma turned into state data
- institutional weaponization
- Chara's struggle against definitions imposed on her
- the gap between what the system predicts and what she chooses to be
