# Soul Fusion

Soul fusion is the process by which multiple soul essences are integrated into a single host. In _Fractured Hope_, it is not a clean power-up. It is an internal war.

## Chara's Fusion

[[characters/chara|Chara]] undergoes the main fusion sequence in Arc 2 under [[characters/axel|Axel]]'s supervision using [[characters/raiden|Raiden]]'s research. The six-human-soul compound is introduced first, then Hate later.

The fused soul set includes:

- Justice
- Patience
- Bravery
- Perseverance
- Integrity
- Kindness
- Hatred

Her Determination acts as the vessel holding the rest together.

## Costs

The story is explicit that the process is irreversible and dangerous. Axel lists risks including:

- soul fracture
- complete personality dissolution
- negative traits overwhelming the host identity

Later, the practical cost becomes clear: using fused traits burns memory, especially memory tied to Raiden.

## Final Step

The last fusion, involving Hate, changes the rules of the story. It also severs the reset anchor permanently. After that, there are no more retries.

## Why It Matters

Fusion is the story's central escalation mechanic, but it is also one of its clearest statements about power: every increase in force makes the self harder to preserve.
