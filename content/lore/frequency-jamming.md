# Frequency Jamming

Frequency jamming is the static-entropy attack and suppression profile associated with [[characters/elias-the-administrator|Elias / the Administrator]] in the Arc 6 closing battle. It operates by introducing entropic static into soul-resonance frequencies — disrupting coordination, communication, and soul-dependent abilities.

## The Library Jammer

The jammer mechanism in [[locations/the-library|the Library]] is the earlier, institutional form of frequency jamming. Chara's Determination-backed memory functions are suppressed by jammer-based confinement during her imprisonment. The Library's jammer is a containment tool — static introduced to prevent specific soul-type operations rather than to attack directly.

Elias's Arc 6 deployment extends this logic into active combat: the same suppression principle, applied offensively.

## Arc 6 Profile

The source material describes Elias's combat profile as including a static-entropy attack — a jamming effect that introduces interference into the frequencies on which soul-based coordination and communication operate. Against opponents like Chara whose abilities are soul-dependent, this creates degraded conditions for everything from Determination-backed persistence to Lumina-dependent emotional clarity.

The pairing of frequency jamming with the [[lore/eclipse-and-lunar-entropy|eclipse state]] active during Arc 6 is consistent: the eclipse degrades light-level and soul-level functions, and frequency jamming degrades the frequencies on which those functions operate. Together they produce maximum entropic pressure.

## Relationship to Memory Fragmentation

The Library jammer's historical effect on Chara — [[lore/memory-fragmentation|memory fragmentation]] — is the long-term consequence of frequency suppression applied over an extended period. Short-term jamming disrupts function; long-term confinement under jammer suppression produces structural fragmentation. The manuscript's treatment of Chara's pre-story memory gaps is partly explained by this distinction.

## Open Questions

> **Unresolved:** Whether Valerius's Lunar Aegis provides any protection against frequency jamming is not confirmed.
