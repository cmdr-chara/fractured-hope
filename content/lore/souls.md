# Soul Types

The soul system in [[index|Fractured Hope]] distinguishes nine naturally occurring types. An artificial tenth exists — [[Jikle]] — created through forced fusion of two incompatible types.

## The Nine Types

| Soul          | Associated trait                          |
| ------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Determination | Will to persist regardless of outcome     |
| Bravery       | Courage under threat                      |
| Justice       | Moral reasoning and proportional response |
| Perseverance  | Endurance under sustained harm            |
| Patience      | Capacity to wait and absorb               |
| Integrity     | Consistency of self                       |
| Kindness      | Care for others at personal cost          |
| Monster       | The soul type native to monster-kind      |
| Hatred        | Sustained destructive drive               |

These are not abstract categories. In the text, soul type shapes behavior, determines what abilities a person can access, and, in the case of fusion, restructures identity at a level that cannot be undone.

## Regeneration

Souls regenerate. The text uses the phrase _"like a starfish"_ — damage heals, threads regrow. This applies to all nine natural types.

[[lore/lumina|Lumina]] does not share this property. See [[lore/lumina|Lumina]] for the distinction.

## Soul Fusion

Fusion is the process of permanently incorporating a second soul's threads into one's own. It is:

- **Invasive** — the process is not passive absorption
- **Irreversible** — no mechanism for reversal is established in reviewed sources
- **Identity-threatening** — the integrated traits exert ongoing pressure on the host's sense of self
- **Costly in use** — each activation of a fused trait draws from specific memory stores. For [[characters/chara|Chara]], each use of her post-Arc 2 fused traits costs a memory of [[characters/raiden|Raiden]]

[[Chara]] undergoes fusion across Arc 2, administered by [[Axel]] via injection. She integrates Justice, Patience, Bravery, Perseverance, Integrity, Kindness, and, in the final fusion, Hatred. The Hatred fusion severs her reset anchor permanently. Axel warns her before this last step. She proceeds.

## Jikle — The Soul of Unmaking

[[Jikle]] is an artificial construct: Hatred fused with Determination in a configuration that should, by the text's own logic, kill its user. [[characters/kager|Kager]] wields it. Why it does not kill him is not explained in reviewed sources.

Its designated function is the erasure of [[lore/lumina|Lumina]] fragments across realities — making it structurally the opposite of recovery. It does not heal or reconstruct. It removes.

The text calls it **The Soul of Unmaking**.

## Determination

[[Determination]] occupies a specific position in the system. It is Chara's native soul type and the source of her reset anchor — the mechanism that, before the Hatred fusion, allowed her to rewind to a save point. It is also [[characters/frisk|Frisk]]'s soul type, though the text notes Frisk's Determination as weaker than Chara's.

The reset anchor is not a conscious tool. It activates under conditions the text does not fully specify. After the Hatred fusion, it is gone.
