# Manifested Notes

Manifested notes are written objects in _Fractured Hope_ that carry emotional or soul-weight beyond their literal content. The most prominent example is the [[lore/lily-note|lily note]]. The manuscript confirms that notes in this story can behave in ways that exceed ordinary written communication — arriving at significant moments, carrying a scent or presence, and functioning as a form of contact across distance or time.

## Definition

A manifested note is not just a message. It is a physical object imbued, deliberately or residually, with the emotional state of its writer. The lily note's arrival is associated with [[characters/raiden|Raiden]]'s scent-signature — meaning the note carries a trace of him that goes beyond words on paper.

The source material confirms this property in the lily note context without specifying a general theory of how note manifestation works. The mechanism — whether it requires deliberate intent, specific soul-type properties, or proximity to Determination-level emotional events — is not detailed.

## The Lily Note

The lily note is the clearest example of a manifested note in the manuscript. It arrives during the pre-story / Arc 4 period. Its sender is not conclusively identified. The note is associated with Raiden's scent-signature, suggesting either that he wrote it or that someone wrote it with access to something of his.

> **Unresolved:** The sender of the lily note is not confirmed in reviewed sources.

→ See [[lore/lily-note|The Lily Note]] for the full artifact page.

## Raiden's Soul-Stabilization Notes

[[lore/raiden-soul-stabilization-notes|Raiden's soul-stabilization notes]] are a related object class — written records that carry practical and emotional weight. They are not described as manifested in the same way as the lily note, but they function as a form of presence after Raiden's death.

## Narrative Role

Manifested notes are one of the story's answers to the problem of absence. Raiden is dead before the main story begins. His presence in the current story operates largely through objects — the locket, the notes, the lily — that carry what he no longer can. The manifested-note mechanic makes those objects more than mementos: they are sites of contact.
