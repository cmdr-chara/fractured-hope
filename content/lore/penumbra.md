# Penumbra

Penumbra is [[characters/valerius|Valerius]]'s shadow-aspect combat ability — the offensive or darker counterpart to his defensive [[lore/lunar-aegis|Lunar Aegis]]. Both abilities are confirmed in the Arc 6 closing battle material. Where Lunar Aegis is a named barrier, Penumbra represents the shadow end of his lunar-aligned kit.

## Mechanics

The source material confirms Penumbra as a distinct ability in Valerius's combat profile, deployed alongside Lunar Aegis during the Arc 6 engagement. The precise mechanics — what form the attack takes, its range, its damage profile — are not detailed in reviewed sources.

The name places it in the shadow-of-the-moon register: a penumbra is the partial shadow at the edge of a full eclipse. This positioning is consistent with the [[lore/eclipse-and-lunar-entropy|Eclipse / Lunar Entropy]] context active during Arc 6.

> **Unresolved:** Whether Penumbra is primarily offensive, disruptive, or serves a different function (mobility, concealment, etc.) is not confirmed beyond its presence as a combat deployment.

## Relationship to Lunar Aegis

Penumbra and [[lore/lunar-aegis|Lunar Aegis]] are Valerius's paired abilities. The pairing suggests a unified lunar combat philosophy: defend with the full moon, strike from the shadow. Whether using both in sequence is costly or whether they are independent is not detailed.

## Eclipse Context

The Eclipse / Lunar Entropy shift active during Arc 6 is the environmental condition under which Penumbra is deployed. A penumbra-type ability operating during an eclipse event carries obvious resonance. Whether the eclipse state amplifies, modifies, or enables Penumbra specifically is not confirmed.
