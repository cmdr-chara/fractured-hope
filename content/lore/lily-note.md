# The Lily Note

The lily note is a written object in _Fractured Hope_ that arrives with [[characters/raiden|Raiden]]'s scent-signature attached. It is confirmed in the pre-story / Arc 4 period material and functions as one of several physical anchors of Raiden's presence after his death.

## Properties

The note is a [[lore/manifested-notes|manifested note]] — it carries weight beyond its literal text. The scent-signature connection to Raiden marks it as something more than correspondence. In a story where material objects carry soul-trace, the lily note's scent association places it in the same category as the [[lore/raiden-locket|locket]]: a site of contact rather than merely a record.

The lily is also an image the manuscript associates with Raiden specifically. The note's name carries that association.

## The Sender

The sender of the lily note is the story's most persistent unresolved identification question.

> **Unresolved:** The sender of the lily note is not conclusively identified in reviewed sources.

The note's scent-signature is Raiden's. This could mean:

- Raiden wrote it before his death, and it arrived after.
- Someone else wrote it using something of Raiden's.
- The manifested-note mechanic imbued it with his presence regardless of who wrote it.

None of these possibilities is confirmed. The wiki tracks this as an open question without resolving it.

→ See [[meta/open-questions|Open Questions]]

## Placement

The lily note appears in the same material cluster as the locket, the soul-stabilization notes, and the Model 500 blueprint — the arc of objects that mark the pre-story period and the relationship between Raiden and Chara. Its position in that cluster is deliberate; it is not incidental correspondence.
