# Paladin Blades

Paladin blades are the edged weapons used by the Arc 6 assault unit — [[characters/marcus|Marcus]], [[characters/martinez|Martinez]], and [[characters/ale|Ale]]. The source material confirms a key mechanical property: their wounds linger. Contact does not end the damage.

## The Lingering Wound Effect

The manuscript describes paladin blades as producing wounds that persist beyond initial contact. The exact mechanism of this effect is not detailed — whether it is a soul-property embedded in the blades, a chemical coating, a magical quality, or something else is not confirmed. The effect itself is confirmed: wounds from paladin blades continue to be a factor after the blow is struck.

This property makes paladin blades tactically significant beyond their function as simple weapons. They are designed to degrade over time, not just to wound in the moment.

> **Unresolved:** The mechanism behind the lingering wound effect is not detailed in reviewed sources.

## Assault Unit Use

The three members of the Arc 6 unit carry paladin blades as part of their coordinated approach. The weapons are consistent with a disciplined, military-trained cell — these are not improvised or personal weapons, but equipment associated with the assault role specifically.

The name "paladin blades" suggests an institutional or ideological origin — "paladin" carries connotations of ordained, hierarchically sanctioned violence. Whether the blades are issued by the broader organization the unit belongs to, or are a unit-specific designation, is not confirmed.

## Interaction with Arc 6

The lingering wound effect is relevant to the Arc 6 battle's attrition dynamics. A closing battle that produces wounds that do not stop is a battle where early contact matters beyond its immediate impact. This shapes the stakes of engagements with [[characters/valerius|Valerius]] and [[characters/chara|Chara]] during the assault phase.
