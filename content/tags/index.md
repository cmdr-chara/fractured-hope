# Tag Index

Quartz v5 generates tag pages automatically. This page exists as a manual entry point for readers who want to browse the site by metadata rather than folder structure.

## Suggested tags to explore

- `character`
- `lore`
- `relationship`
- `theme`
- `meta`
- `spoilers`

## Why tags matter here

Folders capture the canonical shape of the wiki. Tags capture the cross-cutting shape — the pages that belong together even when they live in different sections.
