## A note on tone

The story does not flinch. It contains prolonged captivity and torture, grief that mutates into ideology, characters who do terrible things for reasons that mostly make sense, and no guarantee of recovery. The wiki tries to write about this material with the same steadiness the story brings to it — neither dramatizing for effect nor flattening for comfort.

Pages flag speculation clearly. Canon is quoted or closely paraphrased. Interpretation is marked as such.
