# Library Containment

Library Containment is the name used here for the sequence in which [[characters/chara|Chara]] is imprisoned in [[locations/the-library|the Grand Library]] after escaping [[lore/lab-c7|Lab C7]], classified as **Subject C-1**, and subjected to the institutional logic that produces the **[[lore/the-red-genocide|Red Genocide]]** codename.

This is one of the story's foundational violence-events. It matters not only because it is brutal, but because it is procedural.

## What Happens

After Chara survives Lab C7, she is not rescued into freedom. She is taken to the Grand Library — a white-room containment site using jammer-based confinement. The Library is not treated as symbolic punishment. It is an institutional response to a person the system has already classified as catastrophic.

Inside the Library:

- Chara is held under **C-1** classification
- jammer-based systems suppress her Determination-linked functions
- [[characters/sans|Sans]] manifests the **Judgment Hall**
- Sans crushes her soul there
- [[characters/frisk|Frisk]] locks the door

These facts sit at the center of both Frisk's confession and Sans's long-term guilt.

## The Data Room

The Library is also where Chara finds the file that names what the institutions think she is:

- SUBJECT PROFILE C-1
- EXTINCTION PROBABILITY: 99.4%
- CODENAME: **THE RED GENOCIDE**

She also finds [[characters/raiden|Raiden]]'s research there — the early material that becomes the soul fusion plan. The event therefore contains both the logic of her containment and the seed of the method she later uses to survive.

## Why It Matters

Library Containment is where the story makes one of its hardest claims: the violence done to Chara is not only terrorist violence. It is also state-aligned, rationalized, document-backed violence carried out by people who think they are managing risk.

This is why Frisk's later confession matters, and why Sans's later care cannot be simple. Both are tied to this event specifically, not only to Chara in the abstract.

## Relationship to Lab C7

[[lore/lab-c7|Lab C7]] and the Library are connected but distinct. Lab C7 is the six-month torture site. The Library is the month-long institutional containment site that follows. The manuscript keeps them separate enough that the wiki should not collapse them into one event.
