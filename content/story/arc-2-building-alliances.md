# Arc 2: Building Alliances

Arc 2 is the arc in which [[characters/chara|Chara]] is transformed. She enters it fractured and exits it carrying seven soul types in an irreversible fusion — and without the reset anchor that was her last fallback.

## Major Events

### 2.1 — The Fixed-Point Building

[[characters/axel|Axel]] leads [[characters/chara|Chara]] to the research site [[characters/raiden|Raiden]] had prepared. The building is a temporal fixed point: it resists reality instability in a way that implies deliberate design. It does not shift with timeline flux. Raiden planned for the possibility of his own disappearance.

The facility uses blood recognition for access. This detail matters because it ties the building to a specific person's design rather than generic security protocol.

### 2.2 — The Full Scope of the Research

Inside the [[locations/hidden-lab|hidden lab]], the full picture of Raiden's work emerges. What was framed as personal grief research is revealed to be larger:

- **Soul fusion theory** — the foundation for a procedure that would let Chara carry multiple soul types simultaneously using her Determination as the binding vessel
- **Timeline repair research** — work toward recovering what Lumina fragmentation scattered
- **Anti-Kager preparation** — research that specifically anticipates a confrontation with [[characters/kager|Kager]] and [[lore/jikle|Jikle]]
- **Artificial soul matrices** — research whose full scope is not exhaustively documented in this arc
- **Chara's soul-stabilization notes** — documents containing precise emotional and physical anchoring data written about Chara specifically

Axel presents all of this and explains his own role: he worked covertly for Raiden, retained data across resets through synthesized Determination micro-dosing, and was specifically prepared to administer the fusion if the worst happened.

He delivers his summary of Kager: _"brothers in everything but blood."_

### 2.3 — The Fusion

The fusion is administered across sessions. The six-human-soul compound comes first: Justice, Patience, Bravery, Perseverance, Integrity, and Kindness. Each integrates into Chara's Determination as a distinct presence, not as a seamless upgrade. The traits fight for expression. The process is invasive throughout.

Axel warns her before every significant step. The risks he names include soul fracture, complete personality dissolution, and the possibility that negative traits will overwhelm the host. He also extracts a [[lore/lumina|Lumina]] sample as a safety measure before the final step.

The final injection is **Hatred**. Before administering it, Axel confirms what this will do:

> _"No resets, no reloads. This is your only life now."_

Chara proceeds.

The cost structure reveals itself almost immediately. The first time she uses Justice in combat after the fusion, she loses a memory of Raiden. The exchange is confirmed, not implied: each use of a fused soul trait costs a specific memory.

## Lore Revelations

- **Soul fusion is irreversible.** No recovery mechanism is established in reviewed sources.
- **Memory cost is confirmed as the price of using fused traits.** The connection to Raiden memories specifically is confirmed.
- **The Hate fusion severs the reset anchor permanently.** After Arc 2.3, the story's consequences stop being provisional.
- **Axel's Determination micro-dosing technique** is the mechanism by which he preserved Raiden's plan across timeline shifts.
- **[[lore/artificial-soul-matrices|Artificial soul matrices]] research** exists in the lab material alongside the fusion notes.

## Character Movement

- **Chara** — accepts irreversible transformation, loses the last fallback, immediately starts paying the memory cost
- **Axel** — shifts from gatekeeper to administrator; his visible strain around dangerous research is noted but not explained in this arc
- **Raiden** — fully present only as research and architecture; this arc is the most complete picture yet of what he built

## Thematic Significance

Arc 2 is where the story closes the escape route. The reset anchor was the final structural safety net. Once Chara accepts its loss, every choice from this point forward is permanent. The fusion gives her power at the cost of the very thing she is trying to recover.

The trade is explicit: more force, fewer memories of Raiden. The story starts building the tension here that runs all the way to the closing arc.

## Unresolved

> **Unresolved:** The full extent of Axel's involvement in the artificial soul matrices research — whether he continued the work, simply held the notes, or contributed to their development — is not confirmed.

> **Unresolved:** Axel's visible conflict around dangerous research in this arc is noted in the source material but the source of that conflict is not named directly.
