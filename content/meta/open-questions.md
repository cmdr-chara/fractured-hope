# Open Questions

This page tracks confirmed unresolved points in _Fractured Hope_ — questions that remain genuinely open after full source review. These are not gaps in the wiki; they are gaps in the manuscript material reviewed to date, or deliberate story ambiguities.

For the editorial standard governing these listings, see [[meta/canon-policy|Canon Policy]].

---

## Characters

**Axel's betrayal**
Axel shows visible conflict around dangerous research and exhibits signals consistent with betrayal preparation. His betrayal is not confirmed in reviewed sources.
→ See [[characters/axel|Axel]]

**Marcus's fate**
Martinez is confirmed killed in Arc 6. Marcus is present in the assault unit but his death is not confirmed in reviewed sources.
→ See [[characters/marcus|Marcus]]

**The lily note sender**
A lily note reaches Chara in the pre-story / Arc 4 period. The sender is not conclusively identified in reviewed sources.
→ See [[lore/lily-note|The Lily Note]]

**Raiden's second fragmentation actor**
Raiden's Lumina is fragmented during the Prologue attack. The identity of the second actor in that fragmentation is not confirmed in reviewed sources.
→ See [[characters/raiden|Raiden]], [[lore/lumina|Lumina]]

**Kager's chain of command**
Kager's exact operational relationship to the Arc 6 assault unit and to the "main leader" (possibly separate from [[characters/elias-the-administrator|Elias]]) is not fully defined.
→ See [[characters/kager|Kager]]

**HChara's mechanism of existence**
HChara appears in the main timeline during Arc 5. Her mechanism of existence there — how she sustains herself, whether she has independent agency — is not confirmed.
→ See [[characters/hchara|HChara]]
