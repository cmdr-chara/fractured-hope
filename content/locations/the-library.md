# The Library

The Grand Library is one of the most significant locations in _Fractured Hope_. It is where [[characters/chara|Chara]] was held for a month after escaping [[lore/lab-c7|Lab C7]], where [[characters/sans|Sans]] crushed her soul in the Judgment Hall, and where she found the institutional file that gave her torture a codename: **THE RED GENOCIDE**.

## History

### The Containment

After Chara's escape from Lab C7 at sixteen, she was not freed. She was taken to the Grand Library and imprisoned there for a month. The source material confirms:

- The Library functioned as a **C-1 classification** confinement facility at this point
- **Jammer-based confinement** was in use — devices designed to suppress her Determination and prevent dimensional or timeline movement
- The imprisonment was officially framed within the **Red Genocide** project's institutional logic

[[characters/sans|Sans]] manifested the **Judgment Hall** inside the Library and crushed Chara's soul. [[characters/frisk|Frisk]] locked the door.

These two facts sit together in the manuscript without extensive editorializing. The story does not need to underline them.

### The Data Room

Inside the Library's data room, Chara found the file that named what had been done to her:

- SUBJECT PROFILE C-1
- EXTINCTION PROBABILITY: 99.4%
- CODENAME: **THE RED GENOCIDE**

She also found [[characters/raiden|Raiden]]'s research — the early material that would eventually become the soul fusion plan. This means the Library is, paradoxically, the site where the tool for her survival was waiting alongside the file that justified her imprisonment.

## Physical Description

The source material does not provide extensive architectural description of the Library beyond its function. The "white room" framing appears in the reviewed material and may refer to specific containment areas within the larger structure rather than the entire building. The Library is described as a containment-prison rather than a civilian institution.

## Narrative Significance

The Library appears in [[characters/frisk|Frisk]]'s Arc 4.4 confession by name:

> _"I'm sorry. For the library. For Lab C7."_

She names it first, before Lab C7, suggesting the Library's role in the original betrayal carries its own distinct weight separate from the government facility.

[[characters/sans|Sans]]'s culpability at the Library is central to his character arc. He was part of the institutional system that put Chara there, which is exactly why his later role as reluctant guardian carries the weight it does. He knows what he participated in.

## Relationship to Lab C7

The Library and Lab C7 are related but distinct. Lab C7 was a six-month detention run by a non-government terrorist organization. The Library was an institutional response — government-aligned, Dreemurr-side — imposed _after_ Chara survived and escaped.

The exact relationship between the unnamed "laboratory" and Lab C7 as referenced in different parts of the source material is still not fully mapped.

> **Unresolved:** Whether the Library was a permanent facility or a one-time containment arrangement is not confirmed.

> **Unresolved:** The full scope of what Chara was subjected to during the month in the Library beyond the Judgment Hall event is not detailed in reviewed sources.

> **Unresolved:** The Library's current status — still standing, repurposed, or destroyed — is not confirmed.
