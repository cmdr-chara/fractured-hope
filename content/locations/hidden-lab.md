# The Hidden Lab

The hidden lab is the preserved research facility [[characters/raiden|Raiden]] prepared in anticipation of his own disappearance. It sits beneath a fixed-point building — a structure that resists temporal flux and reality instability in a way that implies deliberate design rather than coincidence. It is where [[characters/chara|Chara]] learns the full scope of what Raiden built, and where [[characters/axel|Axel]] held watch in Raiden's absence.

## The Building

The structure above the lab functions as a temporal anchor. It does not shift with timeline resets. The fixed-point quality is described in a way that implies Raiden either chose the location for this property or engineered it. His failsafe note was placed here — tucked under a flower — as something Chara would be able to find even after a reset.

Access to the lab below requires blood recognition. This ties the facility to a specific person's design and serves as a security layer that is also a statement: the person who built this expected only specific hands to reach it.

## Contents

The lab contains the full infrastructure of Raiden's research:

- **Soul fusion notes** — the foundational research establishing that Chara's Determination is the only viable vessel for carrying all seven soul types simultaneously. This is the documentation that makes Arc 2's fusion procedure possible.
- **Soul-stabilization notes** — precise emotional and physical anchoring data written about Chara specifically. These are recovered and read in Arc 4.
- **Artificial soul matrices research** — additional research material present in the lab; how deep this branch goes and what exactly it proposes is not fully detailed in reviewed sources.
- **The Model 500 blueprint** — listed among Raiden's research materials; catalogued separately as [[lore/model-500-blueprint|a dedicated artifact page]].
- **A damaged holographic device** — a partial message naming [[characters/kager|Kager]] and [[lore/jikle|Jikle]] directly.
- Possibly the reset contingency mechanism itself, or documentation of it.

## Axel's Role

[[characters/axel|Axel]] held access to the lab and waited. He used synthesized [[lore/determination|Determination]] micro-doses to retain memory of Raiden across timeline jumps. His entire function in the story up to Arc 2 is as gatekeeper to this space — someone who could not act on the research alone but could hand it to the right person when she arrived.

The lab is shielded against resets and most magical detection, which is how Axel was able to hold it secure across an indeterminate period.

## Narrative Function

The hidden lab is the story's delivery mechanism for Raiden's plan. Without it, the soul fusion does not happen, the resistance to Kager has no formal architecture, and Chara has no access to the research that makes her eventual transformation meaningful rather than random.

It is also evidence of Raiden's character. He planned for catastrophe before it arrived. He trusted one person — Axel — with the keys. He left precise instructions for someone he might never see again.
