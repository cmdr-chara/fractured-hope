# City Underbelly

The city underbelly refers to the lower infrastructure of war-damaged [[locations/brasilia|Brasilia]] — sewers, basements, ruined ground-level structures, and the kill corridors formed between collapsed buildings. These areas have no single name in the manuscript; the designation covers the terrain staged deliberately for the Arc 6 assault.

## Function in Arc 6

The Arc 6 assault unit — [[characters/marcus|Marcus]], [[characters/martinez|Martinez]], and [[characters/supporting/ale|Ale]] — use this terrain as part of a coordinated tactical approach. The environment is not incidental. The source material describes the ruined city blocks as **kill-box structures**: layouts where lines of sight are controlled, escape routes are funneled, and ambush positions are layered vertically across rooftops, street level, and below.

The closing confrontation with [[characters/elias-the-administrator|Elias / the Administrator]] is staged here after the assault unit contact. The stalemate ending — Chara binding Elias with a HATE + Determination tether — takes place inside this terrain.

## Structural Character

The underbelly is Brasilia's nine-year war made spatial. The parts of the city that were destroyed early and never rebuilt now serve as infrastructure for the assault. Sewers link basements. Ruined upper floors provide high-ground firing positions. Ground-level rubble creates natural chokepoints.

The source material does not describe this terrain as improvised. The assault cell's movement through it is disciplined and pre-planned.

## Relationship to Other Locations

The underbelly is adjacent to and beneath the surface-level [[locations/brasilia|Brasilia]] where Liberado operates. [[locations/the-library|The Library]] and [[locations/hidden-lab|the Hidden Lab]] are institutional spaces separate from this terrain — the underbelly is infrastructure-level, not institutional.

The torture-lab implications present in the manuscript's assault staging are noted in the source gap resolution notes, but a specific torture-lab site within this terrain has not been confirmed as a distinct named location.

## Open Questions

> **Unresolved:** Whether the assault cell pre-established this terrain before Arc 6 or arrived with existing intelligence on Brasilia's war damage is not confirmed.
