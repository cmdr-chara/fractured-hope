# Brasilia

Brasilia is the main urban setting of _Fractured Hope_. The story opens here, most of its named confrontations occur here, and the closing battle of Arc 6 tears through its streets. Nine years of war between humans and monsters have shaped the city into something that is part functioning society, part active combat zone.

## The War Context

The conflict behind the story is not named as anything specific — it is referred to as a war, nine years old at the story's opening. Brasilia is where that war is most visible. [[characters/chara|Chara]] and [[characters/raiden|Raiden]] built [[Liberado]] here. The city is Chara's base of operations throughout the main story.

The handoff documentation describes the setting as _"a war-torn Brasilia"_, and the manuscript supports this: public spaces hold ceasefire events that get attacked, concerts are intelligence-gathering opportunities as much as entertainment, and by Arc 6, major sections are ruined city blocks staged as kill zones.

## Key Events Staged in Brasilia

### The Prologue Attack

The Prologue attack that fragments Raiden's Lumina takes place at a ceasefire event. The exact venue is not exhaustively described in reviewed sources, but it is a public gathering inside Brasilia.

### The Arc 3 Concert

A public concert in Brasilia is the site of the confrontation with [[characters/javier|Javier]] in Arc 3.2. The concert setting is notable because the source material frames it as a suspicious spectacle — artificial peace used as cover. Javier's presence there is deliberate.

### Arc 6 — The Closing Battle

By Arc 6, Brasilia's war-damaged districts become kill-box structures. Sewers, basements, rooftops, and ruined city blocks are the staging ground for the confrontation with the assault unit — [[characters/marcus|Marcus]], [[characters/martinez|Martinez]], and [[characters/ale|Ale]] — and the later confrontation with [[characters/elias-the-administrator|Elias / the Administrator]].

The source material describes these as deliberate tactical terrain rather than incidental locations.

## Liberado's Presence

[[Liberado]] is based in Brasilia. Chara commands it from here. The city is both a home base and a warzone, and the manuscript does not soften that contradiction.

## Institutional Infrastructure

Brasilia is also the location of Dreemurr-side institutional power. The government structures that produced the [[lore/the-red-genocide|Red Genocide]] classification, administered [[lore/lab-c7|Lab C7]], and built the [[locations/the-library|Library]] exist within this city. [[characters/asgore|Asgore]]'s political authority is centered here.

Lab C7 is confirmed as seven kilometers from Chara's home — placing it within or near the city limits.

## Open Questions

> **Unresolved:** The specific geography of Brasilia — district names, the relationship between Liberado's operating zone and the government-controlled areas — is not mapped in detail in reviewed sources.

> **Unresolved:** Whether the concert venue and the ceasefire attack site are in the same district is not confirmed.
